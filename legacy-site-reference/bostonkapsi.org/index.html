FROM THE DESK OF THE POLEMARCH

Dear Brothers, Silhouettes, and Visitors:

As the 36th Boston (MA) Alumni Polemarch I am deeply honored and excited to serve as your new Polemarch. Together, we embark on a journey of leadership, service, and brotherhood. Let us uphold our noble principles and continue to make a positive impact on our communities. Your dedication and commitment are what make our fraternity exceptional.

As we move forward, I encourage open communication, collaboration, and of course -- socialization. Please share your ideas, concerns, and aspirations so that we can work together to strengthen our bond and achieve our collective goals.

Our 35th Grand Polemarch, Brother Jimmy McMikle, has a theme for his administration of "PERFORMANCE OVER POLITICS". The Boston (MA) Alumni Chapter Administration's strategy for delivering on the Grand Polemarch's vision is simple -- we are getting "Back to the Basics"! We will deliver the Grand Polemarch's vision with a "Pathway Forward” of Brotherhood and leveraging the "pillars of enablement" of Undergraduate Support, Service, Communication, Training, Technology, and Risk Management. Our ultimate goal is local chapter success and sustainability. In order for the Grand Polemarch's vision to be realized, we must have a strong and vibrant chapter. Brothers will be able to access a detailed presentation of the 35th Administration's strategy in the Brothers Only section of the website.

Let us march forward with enthusiasm, unity, and a steadfast commitment to our fraternity’s legacy. Together, we will make a lasting difference in our respective communities.

YITB,
Anthony Munro
36th Boston (MA) Alumni Chapter Polemarch

Anthony Munro, Polemarch

Membership Has Its Privileges!

MEMBERSHIP

The Boston (MA) Alumni Chapter of Kappa Alpha Psi Fraternity, Inc. seeks professional men who want to be a part of an organization that gives back to the community. The Chapter is predicated upon and dedicated to the principles of Achievement in every field human endeavor.

The goal of Kappa Alpha Psi Fraternity is not the simple accumulation of members. Our emphasis is on the quality and integrity of membership.

We seek men who serve as positive role models within our communities who can help accomplish the Goals and Objectives of the Fraternity.

Membership Academy Training

Membership in Kappa Alpha Psi is a lifelong dedication to the ideals and lofty purposes of Kappa Alpha Psi, which considers for membership only those aspirants whose personal, social and academic qualifications, are acceptable to both college and Fraternity.
